Hello, I’m Veronica—resident of Belleville, Michigan & founder of Bunny’s Balloons

I’m a wife, mom of two, and a working Mammographer who never lost her love for designing joyful spaces. Before the medical world I was a hairstylist, sculpting color and texture every day. That creative spark never left, so I revived it through Bunny’s Balloons where I bring a unique blend of artistry, empathy, and exactness to every install.

My healthcare career taught me how to stay calm, detailed, and compassionate—qualities that guide every walkthrough, sketch, and onsite build. Each client is special, and I dedicate my full attention to making their celebration truly memorable.
